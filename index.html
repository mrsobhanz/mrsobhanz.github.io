A bit about me

Mechatronics Engineering at Waterloo. My thing is building systems where software, circuits, and physical hardware all have to work together at once — and debugging when they don't.

I've worked on a card-sorting robot, a competition SUMO bot that took first place, and I'm currently on the driveline team for a formula electric car. The through-line is that I like making physical things move correctly.

Outside the lab: EVs, automotive engineering, anything with an interesting control problem underneath it.
